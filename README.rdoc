**This is the backend/api for the Windfollow app.**

**Windfollow** is an accurate and free wind forecast for any spot across the world for 10 days!

Simple and user-friendly app for kitesurfers, surfers, windsurfers, yachtsmen and anyone else who needs information on the wind.

Functionality:

Up to 10 days forecast day by day with a breakdown by hour (GFS 27)
Forecast across the word in the chosen and nearest spots
Interactive spot map showing direction, power and gusts
Displaying and building up reports on the wind in real time
Communication in the exact spots
